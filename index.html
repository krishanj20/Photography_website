About Us

Established in 1965 in Nairobi, Kenya, National Art Studio has a lasting experience in Photography for all occasions.

 

Initially starting as a press photographer, I later created my own photography buisness. After moving to the UK and setting up in London, I became a member of the British Instutite of Professional Photography and Master Photography Association.

 

Despite the development of photos changing massively during my lifetime, The technique and creativity required to take photos is still the same. With my experience behind the camera, I can provide excelent photos.

 

Services

At National Art Studio, we provide a variety of services. If the service you like is not below, please contact us directly.

  • Passport Photos

    We do passport photo's for all countries and we are aware of the different laws regarding each nationalities passport.

  • Framing

    After a special occasion, you may want to put your photos on display at work or at home. We offer a range of frames, please come in for a consultation.

  • Headshots

    For social media, your own websites, or press releases, a stylish headshot is a must. We will provide exactly what you require.

Other Services include: Photo Restoration, Canvas portraits, Graduation photos, Weddings, Photo albums.
